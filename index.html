About

I work with acrylic pour — a technique where paint is mixed with fluid medium and poured, tilted, and coaxed across the canvas. Gravity and chemistry take over from there. The process is equal parts intention and surrender, and every result is genuinely one of a kind.

Based in the UK. Available for commissions and collector inquiries worldwide.
