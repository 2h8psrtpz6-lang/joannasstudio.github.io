Commissions

Interested in a bespoke piece for your home or office? I take a small number of commissions each year. You choose the palette and size — the rest is left to chance.

01

Choose your palette

Warm, cool, or mixed — you guide the colour story. Share references or simply describe the feeling you're after.

02

Pick your size

From intimate studies to large statement pieces. I'll advise on what works well for your space.

03

Receive your piece

Shipped anywhere, carefully packaged. Each piece arrives ready to hang, signed on the back.

About

I work across acrylic pour, palette knife, mixed media, and figurative painting — each series its own exploration of colour, texture, and instinct. The process is equal parts intention and surrender, and every result is genuinely one of a kind.

Based in the UK. Available for commissions and collector inquiries worldwide.
